## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A smartphone running iOS 15+ or Android 8.0+
- Stable internet connection
- A valid email address or phone number for verification

<Callout kind="info">

MyCare requires location services enabled for emergency features. Grant permissions during setup for full functionality.

</Callout>

## Download the MyCare App

Download the app from your device's official store.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">

1. Open the App Store.
2. Search for `MyCare`.
3. Tap `Get` and authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your password.
4. Wait for the download to complete.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">

1. Open Google Play Store.
2. Search for `MyCare`.
3. Tap `Install`.
4. Once installed, tap `Open`.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to set up your profile securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

Open the MyCare app and tap `Sign Up`.

Enter your email or phone number, create a strong password, and tap `Continue`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email/Phone" icon="mail">

Check your inbox or SMS for a verification code.

Enter the 6-digit code in the app to confirm your identity.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Profile Basics" icon="edit-3">

Add your name, date of birth, and emergency contact.

Accept the terms of service and privacy policy.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Use a password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols for better security.

</Callout>

## Complete Onboarding Tour

The app guides you through an interactive tour.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions" icon="shield-check">

Allow access to health data, notifications, and location when prompted.

This enables vitals tracking and emergency alerts.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Health Sources" icon="activity">

Link your Apple Health, Google Fit, or wearables like Apple Watch or Fitbit.

Tap `Connect` for each source.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Join Care Programs" icon="award">

Browse available programs like diabetes management or mental health support.

Select one to enroll.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log Your First Vital Signs

Track your health data manually or via connected devices.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Vitals" icon="monitor-heart-rate">

Tap the `Vitals` tab at the bottom.

Select `Blood Pressure` or `Heart Rate`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Data" icon="plus">

Input realistic values, e.g.:

- Systolic: `120`
- Diastolic: `80`
- Pulse: `72 bpm`

Tap `Save`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Share" icon="share-2">

View your log in the dashboard.

Tap `Share with Care Team` to send securely via `https://api.example.com/vitals`.

  </Step>
</Steps>
