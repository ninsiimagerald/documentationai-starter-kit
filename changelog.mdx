## Overview

MyCare releases updates regularly to improve vital tracking, secure data sharing, and community support features. You receive these updates automatically through your app store, but manual checks ensure you stay current. Review the recent changes below to see enhancements in self-monitoring and care team integration.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable auto-updates in your app settings to get new features and security fixes immediately.
</Callout>

## Recent Releases

<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.3.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Features

- Added advanced vital tracking for blood pressure and oxygen saturation with customizable alerts.
- Introduced trend analysis charts to visualize your health data over time.

## Improvements

- Optimized app performance for faster data syncing with `https://api.example.com/vitals`.
- Enhanced UI for easier navigation in the vitals dashboard.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed sync issues when sharing data across multiple hospital systems.
- Resolved notification delays for emergency bill community requests.

</Update>

## New Features

## Bug Fixes

## Update Your App

Follow these steps to ensure you have the latest version.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check for Updates" icon="download">
    Open your app store and search for MyCare.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install" icon="package">
    Tap "Update" if available. The process takes `<1min`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart App" icon="refresh-cw">
    Force close and reopen MyCare to apply changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Go to App Store > Profile > Update All.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Open Play Store > My apps > Update.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="info">
  After updating, review your settings to enable new vital tracking alerts.
</Callout>
