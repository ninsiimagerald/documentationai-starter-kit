## Overview

You can securely share your vitals and records with your health providers using MyCare.

## Steps

1. Go to the "Care Team" section in the app.
2. Tap "Add Provider".
3. Enter your provider's email or code.
4. Confirm the connection request.
5. Once accepted, your doctor can view only the data you select.

<Callout kind="info">You stay in control. Disconnect any provider at any time.</Callout>
