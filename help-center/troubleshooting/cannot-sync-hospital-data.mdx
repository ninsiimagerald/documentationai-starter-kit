---
title: Cannot Sync Hospital Data
description: Fix issues syncing records from your hospital.
---

## Symptoms

- Recent hospital visits missing in MyCare
- Sync fails with error: "Data source not found"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check hospital connection">
    Go to **Settings â†’ Linked Hospitals** and ensure your hospital appears as "Connected."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect hospital">
    Tap the hospital, select "Reconnect", and log in with your patient credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry sync">
    Tap "Sync Now". Wait 2-3 minutes. If still failing, contact support.
  </Step>
</Steps>
