## Overview

Easily log and monitor your blood pressure, glucose, or other vital stats.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Vitals">
    Go to the **Vitals** tab in MyCare.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add entry">
    Tap the "+" button. Select a vital type (e.g., Blood Pressure).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter values">
    Fill in your readings and tap "Save".
  </Step>
  <Step title="View trends">
    View trends on your dashboard or share with your care team from the **Care Team** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>
